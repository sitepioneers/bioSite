Bio

Valerie is a mother of three, a professional and licensed life and business coach, as well as a certified personal trainer. She is specialized in helping others achieve their personal and business goals by improving their capability, productivity, and consistency. Motivating and mentoring people with self-esteem building, personal development, and business building.

Valerie has a passion for helping others, which is why she chose to be a coach. Her hobbies include spending time with her family, working out, hiking, swimming, and camping.
